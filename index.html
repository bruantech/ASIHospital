Welcome to ASI UKPO Hospitals

ASI Ukpo Hospital started operations in 2013, with the sole objective of making medical services affordable and easily accessible to all. We grew into a full-fledged hospital in 2018. In a continuous quest to meet healthcare demands in the clinic, the ASI Ukpo Comprehensive Cancer Centre (AUCCC) was established as the first privately owned cancer centre in the south-south region of Nigeria.

We offer full diagnostic services ranging from medical imaging to laboratory investigations; in-patient wards; surgery; a blood bank and our cancer centre offering radiotherapy, brachytherapy and chemotherapy services.
