Medical Imaging

ASI Ukpo Hospital is furnished with modern equipment for diagnostic imaging to guarantee accurate diagnosis which is the first step towards proper healthcare. Our medical imaging services include: 3/4D Digital Sonography,Digital X-ray, Echocardiography,Computed Tomography (CT), Magnetic Resonance imaging (MRI), Mammography, Echocardiography.

Laboratory Investigations

We also have as part of our diagnostic services, from routine to specialized investigations for diseases and its effects manned by a team of qualified and experienced medical laboratory scientists and pathologists. Our laboratory is equipped with a fully automated analyzer installed to ensure accuracy, precision, reproducible and reliable results within the shortest turn-around time. Our investigation includes tests in Clinical Chemistry, Microbiology/parasitology, Cytology/Histology, Immunohistochemistry Hematology and blood transfusion, Serotology/Immunology and Endocrinology.

Operating Theatre

With a modular theatre, ASI Ukpo Hospitals is capable of delivering world-class surgical operations with a high margin of safety.

GOPD/IPD

Our team of doctors and nurses dedicated to all out-patient clinic care in the areas of general medicine, Endocrinology, Cardiology, Urology, Orthopaedics, Paediatrics, and dialysis, Gastroenterology, Hematology, Infectious Diseases, Vascular Surgery, Plastic Surgery, General Surgery, Rheumatology, Urology and Preventive diagnostic clinics.

Blood Bank

ASI Ukpo Blood Bank runs an efficient blood banking system that caters for the various clinical transfusion needs of patients by providing readily available blood products such as whole blood, packed cell, fresh frozen plasma, plasma-Cryoprecipitate and Cryosupernatant, platelets-rich and platelets poor plasma, amongst other services.

Dialysis

ASI Ukpo dialysis centre is recognized as one of the best dialysis centre in south-south Nigeria due to its advanced technology and certified experts. It is a centre of excellence, which presents the levels of professional expertise and leadership through a galaxy of eminent faculty. Our dialysis department is committed to treating the patients with utmost care and providing necessary treatment to their kidney related problems.

Antenatal

From the establishment of pregnancy through the different stages of the same, culminating in delivery, ASI Ukpo Hospitals is capable of providing the comfort needed to our teeming mothers and newborns.

Pharmacy

Our pharmacy comprises both the inpatient, outpatient and oncology pharmacy. The pharmacy offers services to all patients on admission and also visiting patients providing the best drugs at the most affordable rate. We also have a comprehensive oncology pharmacy that provides and reconstitutes all chemotherapy drugs in a safe and clean environment (BIOSAFETY CABINET)..

Medical Emergency
