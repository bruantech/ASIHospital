ASI Ukpo Hospital started operations as ASI Ukpo Diagnostic and Medical Centre (ADMC) in 2013, with the sole objective of making medical services affordable and easily accessible to all. In 2016, another diagnostic centre was established in Ogoja, northern Cross River State. The diagnostic centre grew to a hospital in 2018 and the name changed from the diagnostic and medical centre to the current ASI Ukpo Hospitals.

In a continuous quest to meet health care demands in this clinic, the ASI Ukpo Comprehensive Cancer Centre (AUCCC) was established as the first privately owned cancer centre in the south-south region of Nigeria.
