Meet the Founder

ASI Ukpo Comprehensive Cancer Centre is the legacy project of late Brig. Gen. Anthony S. I. Ukpo (Rtd.). As a cancer survivor himself, who also lost three siblings to cancer, he was determined to build an oncology hospital of the highest standard for people in his home state.

After an impressive military career, he continued to play an important role in society. Brig. Gen. Anthony Ukpo was an industrialist with varied interests in hospitality, agriculture, manufacturing, property development and healthcare. He was a recipient of several awards and honours, including Honoris Causa degrees from the University of Calabar, and Ambrose Alli University, Ekpoma in Edo State.

Who We Are

Competence

Our mission is to provide the highest quality cancer care through prevention, research and excellent patient care.

Compassion

We understand the effect of cancer to the overall psychology of our patients, and pledge empathy, respect and kindness to all our patients and caregivers

Care

Our crop of specialists with proven excellence in healthcare delivery, ensuring a high standard of care for each patient.

Commitment

Our commitment is rooted in our accountability and confidentiality for each patient. Our cancer treatment services include: radiotherapy, brachytherapy, chemotherapy, palliative care, super specialty surgical procedures.
